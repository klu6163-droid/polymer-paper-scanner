# Mechanisms in Slide Electrification of Liquid and Frozen Drops on Hydrophobic Surfaces

**arXiv**: [2601.03887](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03887)
**Authors**: Rutvik Lathia, Benjamin Leibauer, Aaron D. Ratschow, Werner Steffen, Hans-J\"urgen Butt
**Categories**: cond-mat.soft

## Abstract

arXiv:2601.03887v3 Announce Type: replace 
Abstract: The microscopic and fundamental origin of slide electrification, where droplets of water move across insulating surfaces accumulating and depositing electrical charges, is still debated. Charge transfer is often attributed to ion transfer at the receding contact line. However, it is still unclear whether ion transfer alone can fully account for the observed charge separation. We examined slide electrification of two polar, self-ionizing liquids (water, formamide) and two non-polar liquids (diiodomethane, bromonaphthalene). By cooling below the melting temperature, we were able to compare this process to tribocharging of the respective frozen components. Despite reduced ion mobility at sub-freezing temperatures, the frozen polar compounds continue to accumulate significant charge. Non-polar liquids exhibit lower charging (<25% of polar liquids) and nearly identical charging behaviour in both their liquid and frozen phases on five different substrates. Since non-polar liquids contain few free ions, these observations indicate an alternative charging mechanism, which could be electron transfer. Our findings suggest that slide electrification operates through at least two mechanisms, with the dominant charge transfer pathway shifting between ions and electron transfer depending on the electronegativity, phase, and temperature.

# 论文快速阅读笔记

## 一句话总结  
通过对比极性与非极性液体及其冷冻相的滑移带电行为，发现滑移电荷分离至少存在离子和电子两种转移机制，其中非极性液体中电子转移占主导。

## 研究问题  
滑移带电（微滴在绝缘表面运动时的电荷积累与转移）的根本机制是什么？仅靠离子转移能否完全解释观测到的电荷分离？

## 核心方法  
- 实验测试四种液体：两种极性自电离液体（水、甲酰胺）和两种非极性液体（二碘甲烷、溴萘）。  
- 将液体冷却至熔点以下，比较液态和冷冻态（固相）的带电行为。  
- 在五种不同疏水基底上测量电荷积累。

## 主要结果  
- 极性液体（液态和冷冻态）均积累显著电荷，但冷冻态离子迁移率降低后仍能带电。  
- 非极性液体带电能力弱（<极性液体的25%），且液态与冷冻态带电行为几乎相同。  
- 非极性液体中自由离子极少，说明存在替代机制——最可能是电子转移。  
- 结论：滑移带电至少有两种机制，主导路径取决于液体电负性、相态和温度。

## 潜在价值  
- 澄清了长期争议：滑移带电并非单一离子转移机制，电子转移在特定条件下起重要作用。  
- 对界面电现象、微流控、防静电材料设计等具有指导意义，推动更精确的电荷耗散模型开发。

# Structural relaxation and stagnation of grain boundary during migration

**arXiv**: [2606.28658](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28658)
**Authors**: Tingting Yu
**Categories**: cond-mat.mtrl-sci

## Abstract

arXiv:2606.28658v1 Announce Type: new 
Abstract: Instability is a major bottleneck in nanomaterials due to grain boundary (GB) activities under thermal or mechanical stimuli. The relaxation of GB will stabilize the properties of materials by structure modification of GBs to lower energy states. However, lack of understanding of mechanisms limits the application of GB relaxation. In this study, we certify that GB can realize relaxation through defect emission including vacancy, twinning and dislocations, etc, which lower the average atomic energy of GB. In particular, we found stagnation and shear-coupling migration accompany the relaxation process, where a lower average atomic energy and lower average atomic volume of grain boundaries can be the reasons for stagnation. In this study, we found when simulated by ramped energy-conserving orientated driving force, under a specific driving force, the grain boundary suddenly stops migrating during the migration process, and as the driving force increases to a certain value, the grain boundary continues to migrate. Even at fixed driving forces, the grain boundary migration process can stall. This phenomenon has been found in many grain boundaries, and it has been found that the reason for the stagnation is the change of the average atomic energy of grain boundaries and the average atomic volume of grain boundaries. The discovery of this stagnation phenomenon is helpful to better understand the migration characteristics of grain boundaries and lays a foundation for improving the strength of materials by designing grain boundary microstructures.

# Structural relaxation and stagnation of grain boundary during migration

> **ArXiv**: [2606.28658](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28658) | **Authors**: Tingting Yu

# 论文快速阅读笔记

## 一句话总结
本文通过原子模拟揭示了晶界迁移过程中的结构松弛与停滞现象，指出缺陷发射（空位、孪生、位错等）是松弛的主要机制，且停滞与晶界平均原子能量和体积变化相关。

## 研究问题
晶界在热或机械刺激下会活动导致纳米材料不稳定，但晶界松弛的机制及其对迁移行为的影响尚不清楚。具体问题包括：晶界如何通过结构修饰实现松弛？松弛过程中是否会出现迁移停滞？停滞的原因是什么？

## 核心方法
- 采用原子模拟方法，使用**斜坡能量守恒定向驱动力**模拟晶界迁移。
- 在不同驱动力条件下观察晶界迁移行为，分析松弛过程中的缺陷发射（空位、孪生、位错）以及原子平均能量和体积的变化。

## 主要结果
1. 晶界通过发射空位、孪生、位错等缺陷实现结构松弛，从而降低晶界的平均原子能量。
2. 松弛过程中伴随**停滞现象**和**剪切耦合迁移**：在特定驱动力下，晶界突然停止迁移；当驱动力增加到某一阈值后继续迁移。
3. 即使在固定驱动力下，迁移过程也可能停滞。停滞的原因归结为晶界平均原子能量和平均原子体积的变化。
4. 该停滞现象在多种晶界中被观察到，具有普遍性。

## 潜在价值
- 深化了对晶界迁移特性的理解，尤其是松弛与停滞之间的耦合关系。
- 为通过设计晶界微结构来调控材料强度提供了理论基础，有助于提高纳米材料的稳定性。

# From microscopic fluctuations to susceptibility spectra: single-molecule relaxation in glassy media

**Categories**: cond-mat.soft

## Abstract

arXiv:2607.02248v1 Announce Type: new 
Abstract: Single-molecule (SM) rotational dynamics of fluorescent probes in polystyrene near the glass transition temperature ($T_g$) are investigated over long times to reconstruct susceptibility spectra. The loss spectrum, commonly recorded using external field-driven (frequency-domain) spectroscopy, such as dielectric spectroscopy, is reconstructed from purely thermal SM rotational fluctuations. The results reproduce time-temperature superposition typically seen in dielectric spectroscopy for materials near $T_g$ and show that the ensemble spectrum is comprised of individual molecular responses to distinct environments.

# From microscopic fluctuations to susceptibility spectra: single-molecule relaxation in glassy media

> **ArXiv**: [2607.02248](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02248) | **Authors**: Siyang Wang, Jaladhar Mahato, Laura J. Kaufman

### 一句话总结
本文通过长时间跟踪单分子旋转涨落，从微观热波动中重建了玻璃态高分子的介电损耗谱，揭示了集合谱由单分子对局部环境的非均质响应构成。

### 研究问题
如何在不施加外场激励的情况下，利用单分子热涨落重构玻璃态材料（接近玻璃化转变温度 $T_g$）的频域介电损耗谱，并理解集合弛豫行为的微观起源？

### 核心方法
对嵌入聚苯乙烯（近 $T_g$）中的荧光探针进行长时间单分子旋转动力学追踪；将其热涨落转化为等效的频域响应，从而重建原始由外场驱动谱学（如介电谱）才能获得的损耗谱。

### 主要结果
- 重建的损耗谱成功再现了介电谱中常见的**时温叠加（time-temperature superposition）** 现象。
- 集合谱由不同单分子对各自微观环境的独立弛豫贡献叠加而成，证明了玻璃态中非均质动力学的微观图像。

### 潜在价值
- 提供了一种无需外部激励便能获取材料弛豫谱的新途径，可避免电场干扰或电极效应。
- 直接验证了玻璃态动力学中“单分子环境异质性”是宏观介电响应谱形的根本来源，有助于完善玻璃化转变理论。

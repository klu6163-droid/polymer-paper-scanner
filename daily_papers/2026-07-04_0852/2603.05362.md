# PolyRapid: Automated High-Throughput Screening of Polymers Using a Computational Workflow

**arXiv**: [2603.05362](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05362)
**Authors**: Lois Smith, Samuel Ericson, Vittoria Fantauzzo, Chin Yong, Paola Carbone et al.
**Categories**: cond-mat.mtrl-sci

## Abstract

arXiv:2603.05362v3 Announce Type: replace 
Abstract: High-throughput computational screening of polymers offers a powerful way to address the imbalance between the vast number of polymers synthesised for diverse applications and the relatively small subset that can be studied using atomistic simulations. This work presents PolyRapid: an automatic workflow designed to enable the rapid and efficient screening of an extensive polymer library. In this work it is deployed on a library of 103 homopolymers. The workflow integrates an automated annealing protocol with adaptive control, allowing for reproducible simulations with minimal human intervention and minimisation of the computational cost. To this end, we test a number of quantitative conformational and energetic convergence metrics. It achieves equilibration in 95% of systems within four 30 ns annealing cycles, and 100% within nine annealing cycles saving, for our dataset, more than 2500 hours of compute compared to a fixed equilibration protocol. The simulation results are compared with experimental data and compiled into a publicly available repository which reports polymer temperature, tacticity and crystallinity. The availability of a homogenous large set of simulations enables the adoption of machine learning approaches for a variety of tasks. We exemplify this possibility by proposing rapid machine-learning-based method to predict the (computed) polymer density (F1-score = 0.91) and (experimental) glass transition temperature (F1-score = 0.76), using both chemical fingerprint representations and physically meaningful MD-derived descriptors.

```markdown
# PolyRapid 论文快速总结

## 一句话总结
该论文提出了一个名为 PolyRapid 的自动化计算工作流，用于对聚合物库进行高通量筛选，在 103 种均聚物上实现了快速平衡，并利用模拟数据结合机器学习预测聚合物性质。

## 研究问题
如何高效、自动化地对大量聚合物进行原子级模拟筛选，以解决实验合成与模拟研究之间的规模不匹配问题？

## 核心方法
PolyRapid 工作流集成了一个带有自适应控制的自动退火协议，能够以最小的人工干预和计算成本重复进行模拟。它使用定量构象和能量收敛指标来判定平衡状态。

## 主要结果
- 在 4 个 30 ns 退火周期内，95% 的体系达到平衡；9 个周期内达到 100% 平衡。
- 与固定平衡协议相比，为数据集节省了超过 2500 小时的计算时间。
- 模拟结果与实验数据进行了比较，并整合到公开存储库中，包含温度、立构规整度和结晶度信息。
- 基于化学指纹和 MD 衍生的描述符，用机器学习预测聚合物密度（F1=0.91）和实验玻璃化转变温度（F1=0.76）。

## 潜在价值
PolyRapid 为聚合物高通量筛选提供了一个可复现、低成本的自动化框架，同时生成均质的大规模模拟数据集，有助于机器学习模型在聚合物性质预测中的应用，加速新材料设计。
```

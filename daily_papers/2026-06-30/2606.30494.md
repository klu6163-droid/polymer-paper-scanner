## Abstract

arXiv:2606.30494v1 Announce Type: new 
Abstract: Herein it is shown spontaneous symmetry breaking in supramolecular aggregates of D4h-symmetric zinc phthalocyanine (ZnPc). Ab initio density functional theory calculations at the M06/DGDZVP level reveal that intermolecular interactions induce a subtle relaxation of the macrocyclic framework, producing a characteristic red shift of the aza-bridge (C-N-C) stretching mode and symmetric stretching of the pyrrole ring. Confocal Raman Optical Activity measurements further reveal a negative Cotton effect at 1505 cm^-1 and 1338 cm^-1, providing evidence of emergent supramolecular chirality. Our findings identify pristine ZnPc as a model system for spontaneous self-assembled chiral symmetry breaking in molecular condensates and suggest new opportunities for chiroptical, spin-selective, and quantum functional materials.

# Spontaneous Symmetry Breaking and Emergent Helicity in Achiral D4h-Symmetric Zinc Phthalocyanine Condensates

> **ArXiv**: [2606.30494](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.30494) | **Authors**: Bruno S. Zanatta, Giovani B. de Oliveira, Marcos E. G. Carmo, Antonio Eduardo H. Machado, Thiago F. Silva

# 论文快速总结

## 一句话总结
本文通过理论计算和拉曼光学活性实验，证明了D4h对称的锌酞菁超分子聚集体中发生自发对称性破缺并涌现出超分子手性。

## 研究问题
- 对称性较高的分子（D4h对称）在凝聚态中是否能自发出现手性？
- 分子间相互作用如何诱导结构弛豫并产生手性信号？

## 核心方法
- 采用M06/DGDZVP水平的密度泛函理论（DFT）计算，分析分子间相互作用对大环框架的影响。
- 使用共焦拉曼光学活性（ROA）测量，探测特定波数（1505 cm⁻¹和1338 cm⁻¹）的Cotton效应。

## 主要结果
- 分子间相互作用使锌酞菁大环框架发生细微弛豫，导致氮桥（C-N-C）伸缩模和吡咯环对称伸缩出现特征性红移。
- 在1505 cm⁻¹和1338 cm⁻¹处观察到负的Cotton效应，证实了涌现的超分子手性。

## 潜在价值
- 将纯锌酞菁确定为自发自组装手性对称性破缺的模型系统。
- 为手性光学、自旋选择性以及量子功能材料的设计提供新思路。

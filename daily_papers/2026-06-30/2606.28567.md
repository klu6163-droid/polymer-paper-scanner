# Ultrafast directed transport via energy recuperation in non-Markovian systems

**arXiv**: [2606.28567](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28567)
**Authors**: Mateusz WI\'sniewski, Jakub Spiechowicz
**Categories**: cond-mat.soft

## Abstract

arXiv:2606.28567v1 Announce Type: cross 
Abstract: A recent pioneering experiment [Nat. Commun. 16, 10114 (2025)] demonstrated that a driven overdamped colloidal particle in a harmonic trap immersed in a viscoelastic fluid can recuperate energy dissipated into the surrounding bath and convert it into useful work. In this article we considerably extend the original predictions. In particular, we show that energy recuperation is a generic feature of non-Markovian systems both in and out of equilibrium, even as simple as a free Brownian particle. Moreover, we demonstrate that inertia alone, even in the strong damping regime, can lead to this effect despite the absence of any external forcing. These results suggest that energy recuperation can be ubiquitous in nature and it may be the modus operandi of various phenomena in setups with memory. We show that this novel mechanism of energy recovery is the source of memory-induced ultrafast directed transport of a particle in a periodic potential in which it almost attains its top speed corresponding to the system with no energy barriers. Our results may answer from the fundamental point of view the question why the cytosol, the intracellular fluid in biological cells, is viscoelastic.

# Ultrafast directed transport via energy recuperation in non-Markovian systems

# 论文快速阅读笔记

## 一句话总结
本文通过理论分析表明，能量回收是非马尔可夫系统的普遍特征，并揭示了该机制可实现周期性势场中粒子的超快定向输运。

## 研究问题
- 能量回收（从耗散能量中回收有用功）现象是否仅存在于特定实验条件，还是非马尔可夫系统的普遍特征？
- 能量回收能否驱动超快定向输运？其物理机制是什么？

## 核心方法
- 基于非马尔可夫动力学理论，分析不同系统（自由布朗粒子、受驱过阻尼粒子）中的能量回收行为。
- 考虑记忆效应和惯性效应，研究能量回收对粒子输运速度的影响，并与无能垒系统的极限速度对比。

## 主要结果
- 能量回收是非马尔可夫系统的通用特征，既存在于平衡态也存在于非平衡态，甚至对自由布朗粒子也成立。
- 即使在强阻尼条件下，仅靠惯性（无需外部驱动力）即可引发能量回收。
- 该机制导致周期性势场中粒子实现近乎无势垒的极限超快定向运动。
- 可能从根本解释细胞胞质（细胞内液）为何具有黏弹性。

## 潜在价值
- 揭示了记忆耗散系统中能量转化的新视角，对理解生物细胞内输运、微尺度操控等具有启发意义。
- 为设计无需持续外部驱动的超快定向输运系统提供理论基础。
